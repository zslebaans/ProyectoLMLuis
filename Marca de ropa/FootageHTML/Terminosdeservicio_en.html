Welcome to DZeta

Since our founding, we have focused on offering our customers high-quality clothing and products designed to fit their active and modern lifestyle. Whether you are looking for running shoes, t-shirts for your daily life or accessories to complement your look, at DZeta we have what you need.

Shipping and returns

At DZeta we make sure that your shopping experience is as simple and pleasant as possible. Here we provide you with all the information about our shipping services and return policies:


Shipping

We make national and international shipments. Estimated delivery time depends on your location, and you will be able to track the status of your order through our online tracking system. In addition, we offer free shipping on orders over €100.

Returns

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the products within 30 days of receiving your order. Please make sure the items are in their original condition and with all tags so we can process your return.

How to make a return?
  • 1. Access your account on our website and select the "My Orders" option.
  • 2. Choose the order you want to return and select the items you want to return.
  • 3. Print the return label we will provide and package the items carefully.
  • 4. Send the package using the courier service indicated on the label.

If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact our customer service team.
